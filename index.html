GAMEPLAY

Each round, you will be presented with the plot of a random movie. Buzz in using the key displayed beneath your player (Q for player 1, M for player 2). After buzzing, click on the correct film. If you are right, you will get a point! If not, the other player will have a chance! If both players answer incorrectly, no one gets points! First to 5 points wins!
