How to Play!

the game lasts 10 rounds. each round, you will be presented with the plot of a random movie. Buzz in using the key displayed beneath your player. After buzzing, click on the correct film. If you are right, you will get a point. If not, the other player will have a chance. If both players answer incorrectly, no one gets points. First to 5 points wins!
